About Trolls

Originally developed by Thomas Dam, a fisherman from Denmark, these lovely creations were hand crafted for his daughter play with in 1959, and were later marketed to the public. After years, these toys became very popular within North America becoming a major fad from 1963-1965, and theu made another comeback during the 1980's and early 1990's

Now you can collect them once again, give the gift of these unique treasures, and let your children experience the joy and excitement you once felt.
